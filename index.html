The Brief

Build a consumer-facing landing page for OooWee Deals — a curated app-rewards program — that converts cold traffic into qualified weekly-newsletter signups across food, coffee, crypto, gas, and streaming verticals.

My Approach

Designed a single-scroll landing with a high-contrast hero (value prop + dual-CTA: browse vs. subscribe), a tappable category preview (Fast Food · Coffee · Pizza · Crypto · Gas · Streaming) and four anchor-brand cards per category with reward-mechanic shorthand (10 pts / $1, Free Fridays, 250 pt redemptions). Built end-to-end in GoHighLevel — funnel canvas, CTA wiring, form capture, automation pipeline.

Surface Stats
  • 40+ apps curated across 8 reward verticals
  • $500+ projected annual savings claim — anchors the value prop above the fold
  • 2 primary CTAs split-tested: Browse All vs. Get Weekly Drops
  • 8 category tabs with 4 brand cards each — 32 reward mechanics surfaced

Performance metrics (visits · opt-in rate · CPL · subscriber growth) coming in the next portfolio revision with full client permission.
