Live Funnel ↗
What It Does

A consumer rewards landing page that turns deal-hunters into weekly newsletter subscribers. Visitors find free food, fuel perks, and streaming rewards across the apps they already use — and sign up to keep getting the best ones in their inbox every week.

How It Captures Leads

One scrollable page does the whole job: a clear promise at the top, six taste-based categories below (Fast Food, Coffee, Pizza, Crypto, Gas, Streaming), and a hand-picked shortlist of brands inside each. Two clear actions give every visitor a reason to convert — browse the rewards now or subscribe for weekly drops. Built and live in GoHighLevel.

By the Numbers
  • 40+ apps hand-picked across 8 categories
  • $500+ in yearly savings promised on the hero
  • 2 conversion paths — browse instantly or join the newsletter
  • 32 brand rewards surfaced (4 per category × 8 categories)

Live performance — visits, signups, cost per lead, subscriber growth — to be added once OooWee approves the disclosure.

Live Funnel ↗
What It Does

A done-for-you booking system for HVAC company owners. Replaces guesswork marketing with one funnel that fills the calendar with homeowners ready to schedule an estimate — built for shops doing $500K–$10M a year.

How It Captures Leads

The page walks HVAC owners through the four services that fill their pipeline: a custom landing page, email blasts to verified homeowner lists, Google Ads + Local Service Ads, and AI that books estimates straight onto the dispatch calendar. Two free tools — an instant ROI calculator and a free growth audit — turn casual visitors into booked calls. Built and live in GoHighLevel.

By the Numbers
  • 14 states covered for HVAC estimate bookings
  • 4 services bundled into one done-for-you offer
  • 14d from signup to fully live funnel
  • 2 free tools that turn visitors into leads — ROI calculator + growth audit

Live performance — booked estimates, cost per lead, close rate — to be added once the OooWee team approves the disclosure.
